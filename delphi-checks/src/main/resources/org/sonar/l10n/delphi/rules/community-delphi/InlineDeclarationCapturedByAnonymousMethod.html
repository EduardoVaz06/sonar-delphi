Up until Delphi 10.4.2, there are compiler bugs related to inline variables captured by anonymous methods.

- Inline variables will not have their lifetimes extended until the anonymous method goes out of scope. (Fixed in 10.4.1)
- Inline variables with managed types will not have their reference count updated when the anonymous method goes out of scope, causing memory leaks. (Fixed in 10.4.2)

See:
- RSP-26666
- RSP-29564
- RSP-31864
